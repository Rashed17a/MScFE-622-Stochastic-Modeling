## Topic

**Black-Scholes Option Pricing & Implied Volatility** — Covers the application of the Black-Scholes model to price European call and put options and compute implied volatility from market data.

## Key Topics

- Black-Scholes option pricing formula
- Implied volatility calibration from market prices
- Option Greeks: Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta
- Volatility surface construction
- Numerical methods for root-finding (Newton-Raphson, Bisection)
